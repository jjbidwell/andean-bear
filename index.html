The Magnificent Andean Bear

The Andean bear (also known as the spectacled bear), is a bear species originating in the Andes Mountain range of South America. It's range typically spreads from Venezuela to the very northern tip of Argentina

The bear is easily identified by the distinct pattern on its face. Often times, this pattern loosely resembles a pair of glasses, hence the name "spectacled bear".

Like many bears, Andean bears have a diverse diet, however they tend to be more herbivorous than other species of bear because of the environment they live in.
